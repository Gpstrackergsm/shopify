We are excited to announce that Embracing Our Fragmented Selves by Dr. Janina Fisher is now available in our library.

Trauma fragments the self, leaving behind parts of us that carry fear, rage, shame, hopelessness, and despair. In this compassionate workbook, Dr. Fisher offers a clear and structured approach to working with these parts and restoring a sense of inner harmony.

Grounded in parts work and sensorimotor psychotherapy, this workbook teaches you to:

  • 🔍 Understand your symptoms as expressions of different parts
  • 🤝 Notice, unblend from, and befriend traumatized parts
  • 🛡️ Recognize self-destructive behaviors as protector parts at work
  • 💜 Offer reparative experiences to wounded young parts
